Company fees and other Costs

If you decide to work with us to process your documents to Canada, you would need to pay us 5 million sums at the beginning of the process and 5 million sums when you get your VISA.

Your other costs
  1. Visa fee – C$150 +$85
  2. Application or Admission fee. This depends on a University you choose. Most Universities deduct this fee as you work with FINO. On average though the admission fee would cost at minimum C$100 to up to C$500.
  3. Your living costs largely depend on location and the lifestyle you follow. We would say it could be around from C$ 600 minimum to up to C$ 1000 per month.
  4. Tuition fees you pay mostly before you are issued a visa. For more details you must visit our office.

*For full University list you must visit our office any time without any prior registration Monday to Saturday 9:00 to 18:00
